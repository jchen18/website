ABOUT  ME

 

 

I’m designer, with skills in illustration, motion, and UI design. I’ve freelanced and worked with companies including Slack, Buzzfeed, Yik Yak, and many more. Making things look good is my passion. I love playful animations, interesting new illustration styles, and weird af UI patterns. All Iconography & Photography on this site is original content by me.

 

I’m decently tall, ok at snowboarding, extremely enthusiastic about ramen, and a stay at home dad (for my dog.) I casually take pictures occasionally. I’m a full time student, currently pursuing an BFA in graphic design. Get @ me. 

WHAT I DO

 

ILLUSTRATION

 

 

Mobile, web, print, you name it, I’ll create some sweet illustrations for it. I start by doing a bit of research and some quick sketches, then bringing things to the computer to make it real. I love taking complex things, abstracting them, and making everything simple.

 

UI DESIGN

 

 

The quote above is something I read awhile back and has stuck with me ever since. I think about it every time I come across a website or an app or a program that is a usability nightmare. All I want to do is fix it, and make it work and look better.

 

ANIMATION

 

 

I do animation on all sorts of different things. I’ve loved motion design for years, both in UI and in random, fun animations. I like bright colors, smooth transitions, and crisp movements. I do most work in Adobe Illustrator & After Effects.
